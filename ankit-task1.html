About the Campaign 🐾

Welcome to the Pet-Friendly City Campaign – an initiative powered by passionate volunteers to make urban spaces safer, kinder, and more joyful for our furry friends! 🐶🐱

We work with communities, local authorities, and animal lovers to build a world where every stray has shelter, food, love, and a future.

🐕 Our Mission

To create a city where every tail wags freely and safely.

🐾 Our Impact

Over 1,000 adoptions, 200+ volunteers, and endless paw prints of love.
